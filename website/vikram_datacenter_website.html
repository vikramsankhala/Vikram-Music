04 — Build Notes

Buying Logic

01

Keep Ubuntu Linux and all active development workloads on the NVMe SSD for responsiveness.

02

Reserve the 8 TB HDD for backups, cold archives, datasets, rendered exports, and media storage.

03

For music work, a USB DAC or audio interface is preferable to onboard analog audio for cleaner line-out to the amplifier.

04

For ROS 2 and container-heavy work, choose a mini-PC with upgradeable RAM and at least one additional storage path if possible.

05

If the chosen mini-PC does not support an internal 3.5" HDD, use the 8 TB drive in an external USB enclosure or NAS-style dock.

06

Remaining budget can be kept as reserve for UPS, better cooling, Ethernet switch, or a second SSD later.
