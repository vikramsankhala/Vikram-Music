Build Notes & Procurement Logic SEC-3.0
N1 Keep Ubuntu Linux and all active development workloads on the NVMe SSD for responsiveness.
N2 Reserve the 8 TB HDD for backups, cold archives, datasets, rendered exports, and media storage.
N3 USB DAC preferred over onboard analog for cleaner line-out to the amplifier and better studio monitoring path.
N4 For ROS 2 and container-heavy workloads, choose a mini-PC with upgradeable RAM and at least one additional storage path.
N5 If chosen mini-PC does not support internal 3.5" HDD, place 8 TB drive in external USB enclosure or NAS-style dock.
N6 Remaining budget reserved for UPS, better cooling, Ethernet switch, or a second SSD. Verify DDR4 vs DDR5 before ordering RAM.
