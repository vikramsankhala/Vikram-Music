Business Architecture

The Lease & Operate
Revenue Engine

AeroLease deploys drone fleets to farmer cooperatives under a long-term lease agreement. The cooperative becomes the local operator, monetizing usage on a pay-per-minute basis—creating a self-sustaining ecosystem with compounding revenue.

01

AeroLease → Cooperative: Fleet Lease

AeroLease leases a fleet of precision agriculture and multispectral drones to the cooperative on a fixed monthly lease rate. Maintenance, software updates, and insurance are bundled. Typical lease term: 36–60 months.

02

Cooperative → Farmer: Pay-Per-Minute

The cooperative operates the drones and charges member and non-member farmers on a pay-per-minute flight basis. Members receive discounted rates; non-members pay commercial rates. Revenue is collected via mobile wallet or prepaid tokens.

03

Data Intelligence Layer

Every flight generates geospatial, multispectral, and LiDAR data uploaded to the AeroLease cloud. AI models produce actionable reports (pest maps, yield forecasts, spray prescriptions) delivered to farmers via a mobile app.

04

Expansion: Industrial Verticals

The same fleet infrastructure is leveraged for geological surveys, mine monitoring, forest fire suppression, and environmental compliance—serviced directly by AeroLease or through certified regional operators on revenue-share agreements.

Verticals 02–05

Industrial &
Environmental Verticals

The same drone infrastructure scales into four high-value industrial verticals, each generating independent revenue streams and serving critical public and commercial needs.

⛏️

Geological Surveys & Mine Monitoring

LiDAR-equipped drones conduct centimeter-accuracy topographic surveys, volumetric stockpile calculations, and real-time structural mapping of open-pit and underground mine environments. Continuous environmental compliance monitoring tracks dust, effluent, and subsidence.

  • LiDAR 3D point cloud mapping at 200–400 pts/m²
  • Automated stockpile volumetrics (±0.3% accuracy)
  • Slope stability and deformation monitoring
  • Mine drainage and water contamination surveys
  • Tailing dam integrity inspection with thermal
  • Real-time dust particulate and gas sensing
  • Monthly compliance reports for MOEF & IBM

Flight rate: ₹18–28 / min  |  Survey package: ₹45,000–1,20,000 per km²

🔥

Forest Fire Detection & Suppression

Thermal and multispectral drone fleets provide 24/7 early fire detection across forest blocks. AI fire spread modeling provides real-time containment vectors to ground crews. Specially-equipped drones deploy fire retardant or water mist in the initial suppression window.

  • Thermal hotspot detection at 2km+ range
  • AI fire perimeter mapping updated every 5 minutes
  • Wind-adjusted spread prediction model
  • Retardant payload delivery drones (20L capacity)
  • Coordination with Forest Dept. command systems
  • Post-fire biomass loss and erosion risk assessment
  • Tiger reserve & corridor protection integration

Patrol subscription: ₹2.5–5L / month per 500km² block  |  Incident response: ₹1,200 / hr

🦅

Wildlife & Ecological Support

Low-noise drone platforms provide non-invasive wildlife census capabilities, anti-poaching patrol, and habitat mapping for conservation NGOs and state forest departments. Real-time thermal feeds integrate with ranger deployment systems.

  • Mammal population census via thermal drone grids
  • Anti-poaching night patrol with AI anomaly alerts
  • Corridor and habitat fragmentation mapping
  • Invasive species detection (Lantana, water hyacinth)
  • Nesting site monitoring with minimal disturbance
  • Biodiversity indices from canopy structure analysis
  • Integration with WII and NTCA databases

Conservation retainer: ₹1.8–3.5L / month  |  Census mission: ₹28,000 / day

🌊

Air & Water Contamination Monitoring

Multi-sensor drone platforms carrying electrochemical gas arrays, particulate sensors, and water sampling payloads provide industrial facility compliance monitoring, river basin health surveys, and urban air quality mapping at a fraction of traditional monitoring cost.

  • Real-time SO₂, NOₓ, CO, PM2.5 / PM10 sensing
  • Automated industrial effluent source tracking
  • River and lake water sampling (pH, DO, turbidity)
  • Heavy metal plume mapping with GPS tagging
  • Urban heat island and microclimate mapping
  • CPCB and state PCB compliance data pipelines
  • 3D atmospheric dispersion modelling

Compliance monitoring: ₹22,000 / survey day  |  Annual retainer: ₹8–18L per facility
